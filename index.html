Why We Focus On Water ? Every system in the human body counts on water to function. Water transports oxygen .to your brain, which allows it to communicate with the rest of the body. Even the slightest lack of hydration can reduce your concentration and cognitive skills, impairing your ability to think clearly and reach maximum performance.
